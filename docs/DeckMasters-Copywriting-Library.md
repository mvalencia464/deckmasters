*Ready-to-paste short-form PAS for each major service page.*

---

### Structural Deck Repair
**P:** Your deck's surface looks fine, but something underneath feels wrong. Maybe it's the flex when you walk across it. Maybe it's the post that doesn't sit square anymore.
**A:** Structural problems don't announce themselves until they fail. And in Alaska, the freeze-thaw cycle works on every weakness in your frame every single winter.
**S:** Deck Masters goes below the surface. They find what's actually wrong, explain it in plain language, and fix the framing — not just the cosmetics.

---

### Deck Board Replacement
**P:** A few boards have gone gray, soft, or started cupping at the edges.
**A:** Soft boards are where water gets in. Once it's in the frame, you're not replacing boards — you're replacing structure.
**S:** Deck Masters replaces boards with materials matched to what you have, or helps you upgrade to composite that won't have this problem again for 25 years.

---

### New Deck Construction
**P:** You have a backyard and no deck. Or you have a slab that never became what you imagined.
**A:** Every summer you put it off is a summer you didn't use your outdoor space. And in Alaska, that's a real cost.
**S:** Deck Masters takes you from site visit to finished build — permit included. Most homeowners say they should have called years earlier.

---

### Multi-Level Decks
**P:** Your yard has elevation changes, or you want to connect a first-story door to a second-story space, or you've got a vision that involves more than a flat platform.
**A:** Multi-level builds require engineering precision. A crew that guesses on the framing will give you a deck that looks off and settles wrong.
**S:** Deck Masters has built complex, multi-level systems — including second-story wraparound replacements with new first-floor connections. They know how to make the levels feel intentional, not just stacked.

---

### Siding Replacement
**P:** Your siding is overdue. Maybe it's showing its age, or a deck replacement revealed rot underneath.
**A:** Compromised siding is a moisture pathway into your wall assembly. Left alone, it creates the conditions for the mold problem that surprises you during the next renovation.
**S:** Deck Masters replaced siding on a complex, odd-angled home — measuring and cutting everything so the new materials fit precisely, including adjusting deck boards where the gap wasn't perfectly parallel. That's the attention to detail level.

---

### Permit Acquisition Assistance
**P:** You've heard permitting in Anchorage is slow, unpredictable, and can derail a project mid-build.
**A:** A contractor who doesn't handle permits hands that problem to you. And a municipality hold doesn't pause the weather.
**S:** Deck Masters handles permitting as part of the job. They've worked with the Muni on complex projects and have a track record of getting it done — while keeping your build on schedule.
