Scope Of Work
Time & Material To Construct Approximately 422 sq ft Decking Including Steps With Pressure Treated Material As Framing. The Main Deck Design Is Above. This Would Include 2x8, 2x6, 4x12, 4x6 Pressure Treated Framing. The Decking Would Be Trex Enhance Decking With A Seamless Picture Frame Design. The Trex Railing With Black Aluminum Balusters Installed On The Inside Of The Deck. The Stairs Would Be Constructed With Pressure Treated Material Attached To A Footer In The Ground. The Stair Treads Would Be The Same Decking As Well. The Stairs Would Have Treated Risers (kick plates). All New Deck Framing Will Be 12" On Center And Fastened With Hangers. This Doesn't Include Any Work On The Existing Framing Of The House Or The Siding. 

Pile Foundation- Steel Piles
Decking- Trex Enhance Decking
Deck Design- Seamless Picture Frame Decking
Railing- Trex Railing With Black Aluminum Balusters
Railing Style- Installed Inside Of The Deck
Deck Height- Built to the bottom of door
Stair Risers (Kick Plate)- Smooth Treated Material
Fascia- N/A

Pergolas:

Back - (Framed with 2"x12")- Clear Plastic Roofing, Cedar Framed, Black Hardware
