# Deck Masters AK: 2026 Comprehensive Advertising Master Plan

## 1. Executive Summary
**Objective:** Capture high-intent leads for deck builds and exterior renovations in the Anchorage area.
**Goal:** Fill the 2026 build calendar with high-value projects (Wraparound decks, Multi-level decks, Structural repairs).
**Primary Strategy:** Deploy a "5-Angle Matrix" creative test on Meta (Facebook/Instagram) while capturing high-intent traffic on Google.
**The Big Idea:** **"The No-Horror-Story Contractor."** (Positioning against the market's biggest weakness: reliability).
**Key Differentiation:** Leveraging "Voice of Customer" data (Relief, Trust, Speed) to counter typical contractor horror stories.

---

## 2. Campaign Architecture

### A. Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)
**Structure:**
*   **Campaign Objective:** Leads (Instant Forms) or Conversions (Website).
*   **Buying Type:** Auction.
*   **Campaign Level:** ABO (Ad Set Budget Optimization) for Phase 1 (7-10 days) to force equal spend, then CBO.
*   **Ad Sets:** 5 Distinct Ad Sets (One per Angle).
    *   *Ad Set 1:* Premium/Luxury (Pride/Status)
    *   *Ad Set 2:* Speed/Efficiency (Time/Regret)
    *   *Ad Set 3:* Trust/Fixed-Price (Fear/Skepticism)
    *   *Ad Set 4:* Smart Investment/Value (Logic/Future-Pacing)
    *   *Ad Set 5:* Relief/Contractor Fatigue (Peace of Mind)
*   **Targeting:** Broad (Anchorage + 25 miles). Let the creative do the targeting.
    *   *Exclusions:* Renters (if possible), 18-24 age group.

### B. Google Ads (Search)
**Structure:**
*   **Campaign 1: Brand Protection**
*   **Campaign 2: High-Intent Service**
*   **Campaign 3: Emergency/Repair** (New)
    *   *Keywords:* "Deck repair anchorage", "Structural deck fix", "Rotting deck support".

## 3. Creative Strategy: The 5 Core Angles

### Angle 1: Premium Outdoor Luxury
**Emotion:** Pride, Status, Envy.
**Target:** High-income homeowners (South Anchorage, Eagle River).
**Hook:** "Turn your backyard into the private resort your neighbors will envy."

### Angle 2: Speed & Efficiency
**Emotion:** Impatience, Regret (Fear of missing summer).
**Target:** Busy professionals, Military families (JBER).
**Hook:** "Don’t Lose Another Alaska Summer to a Half-Finished Project."

### Angle 3: Trust / Fixed-Price
**Emotion:** Fear, Skepticism, Protection.
**Target:** Cautious homeowners, those burned by past contractors.
**Hook:** "The Only Deck Builder in Anchorage With a No-Surprise Guarantee."

### Angle 4: Smart Investment / Value
**Emotion:** Logic, Security, Future-Proofing.
**Target:** Value-conscious, long-term thinkers.
**Hook:** "A Deck Isn’t an Expense. It’s an Asset."

### Angle 5: Relief / Contractor Fatigue (NEW)
**Emotion:** Relief, Exhaustion, Validation.
**Target:** Women 35-55, Project Managers of the household.
**Hook:** "Finally... A Contractor That Makes This Easy."

2.  **Free Frost-Heave Assessment** (For the "Value" angle).
